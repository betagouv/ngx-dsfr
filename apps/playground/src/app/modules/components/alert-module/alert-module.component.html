DsfrAlertComponent

The DsfrAlertComponent purpose is to draw the user's attention on a specific piece of information.

It is based on the Alerte open_in_new component in the DSFR open_in_new .

It has several @Input() properties:

A title for the alert The type will influence the general look of the alert, namely the color and the icon displayed. You can import the AlertType enum in your app to safely provide a value for this property. You can import the ElementSize enum, from the @betagouv/ngx-dsfr library entry-point, in your app, to safely provide a value for this property. Whether or not the alert will be closable by the end-user. Whether or not the alert block will have the role="alert" HTML attribute. For accessibility purposes, it must have it if the alert is supposed to be displayed dynamically, during navigation.
