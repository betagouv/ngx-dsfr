DsfrHeaderComponent

The DsfrHeaderComponent purpose is to represent the header of an application.

It is based on the Header open_in_new component in the DSFR open_in_new .

It has several @Input() and @Output() properties:

The part of the French government ultimately in charge of the web application. It must respect some rules on how to break lines open_in_new . The path on your server to the logo of the operator, the agency within the institution, in charge of the web application. The alternative text for the logo of the operator. Necessary for accessibility: it MUST have at least the text visible on the logo illustration. The name of your web application. A short description of what your application does. The path, corresponding to the Angular Route that you want the user to be redirected to when they click on the institution, operator's logo or your app's name. It will display the autocompletion of the search It will display the search bar component on the right of the header. It emits the search bar value on click on the search button.

We use the DsfrHeaderActionDirective, applied on an ng-template tag, to provide through content projection open_in_new the header actions.

If you need to provide navigation, you can also use the DsfrNavigationComponent via content projection open_in_new .
