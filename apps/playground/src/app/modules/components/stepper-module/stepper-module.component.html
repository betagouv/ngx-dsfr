DsfrStepperComponent

The DsfrStepperComponent purpose is to represent a stepper

It is based on the Stepper open_in_new component in the DSFR open_in_new .

It has several @Input() properties:

The title of the step that the stepper is on. The number of the step the stepper is on. The total number of steps. The title of the next step, it's not required only if the stepper is on the last step (stepNumber = numberOfSteps)
